# Product Requirements Document

## Overview

AI Cash Flow & Treasury Platform for SMEs — treasury intelligence first, ERP expansion later.

## Problem

SME founders and finance teams lack accessible, executive-grade cash visibility without enterprise ERP complexity.

## Target Users

- SME founders
- Finance managers
- Virtual CFOs
- Insolvency and restructuring professionals

## Industries (MVP)

- Construction
- Consulting
- Trade and service businesses
- Insolvency / bankruptcy firms
- Professional services

## Core MVP Capabilities

<!-- TODO: Expand each section during implementation -->

1. Strict CSV/Excel template uploads with validation
2. 13-week weekly rolling cash forecast
3. Treasury rules engine (runway, liquidity score, alerts)
4. Budget vs actual comparison
5. Scenario modelling with manual sliders
6. Executive treasury dashboard
7. AI CFO assistant (conversational, executive tone)
8. Localization-ready UI (English first)

## Out of Scope (MVP)

- Xero / QuickBooks live sync
- Live bank feeds
- Smart column mapping
- Advanced driver-based forecasting
- Low-code workflow builder

## Success Metrics

<!-- TODO: Define beta KPIs -->

- User completes upload → valid 13-week forecast
- Dashboard shows runway and liquidity score
- AI commentary references treasury context accurately
